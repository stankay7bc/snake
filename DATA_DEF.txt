GAMEWORLD is an Object:  
- field:SCENE
- snake:SNAKE  
- food:FOOD  

SCENE is an Object:  
- width:N
- height:N  
- cellDim:N  
dimensions of a playing field

SNAKE is an Array<sPoint>  

  sPoint is an Object: 
  - x:N, x coordinate on a canvas
  - y:N, y coordinate on a canvas 
  - d is either r,l,d,u

FOOD is an Object:
- x:N, x coordinate on a canvas
- y:N, y coordinate on a canvas
- total:N
